Design a minimal, modern, and visually elegant website for an event management company specializing in Indian wedding planning. The design should feel contemporary and upscale while incorporating subtle Indian cultural elements—never overwhelming or traditional-heavy. Think refined, fresh, and culturally inspired.

Overall Style & Mood

Minimalist, clean, and premium

Warm, inviting tone with elegant Indian cultural hints

Avoid clutter—focus on white space, balance, and refined detailing

Blend modern luxury aesthetics with soft cultural motifs

Color Palette

Neutral base: ivory, off-white, warm beige

Accent colors: rich maroon, deep gold, blush pink, emerald, muted saffron

Gold or brass accents used sparingly for sophistication

Pastels can be infused for modern wedding charm

Typography

Modern serif for headings (timeless + premium)

Clean sans-serif for body text (minimal + readable)

Optional calligraphic touches for section titles or initials

Visual Elements

Minimal line-art or subtle patterns inspired by:

mandala geometry

paisley outlines

jaali lattice patterns

mehndi-style motifs (super minimal)

High-quality imagery of Indian wedding decor, outfits, venues (light, bright, aspirational)

Use hero banners with soft gradients, elegant typography, and warm lighting

Layout Requirements

Clean, modular sections

Large hero section with tagline, call to action

Well-spaced service cards with icons

Testimonials carousel

Gallery with minimal borders and soft shadows

Easy navigation with sticky top bar

Key Pages / Sections

Home

Opening hero with a modern Indian wedding ambiance

“Our Services” preview

“Why Choose Us” with icons

Featured weddings

Client testimonials

CTA banner

Services

Wedding planning, decor, logistics, vendor coordination

Each service card modern with culturally inspired accents

Portfolio / Featured Weddings

Story-based layout with minimal film-style frames

About Us

Brand story, mission

Designer/Planner photos in monochrome or soft tones

Contact / Consultation Booking

Clean form with subtle festive border or iconography

Website Feel in One Sentence

A minimal, modern, and culturally elegant digital experience that celebrates Indian weddings without overwhelming the design—luxury, warmth, and subtle tradition.
